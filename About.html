About Resturent

Welcome to our humble restaurant, where the aroma of the past meets the flavors of the present! Here, within walls that whisper tales of our ancestors, we offer you an authentic food and beverage experience that pulses with the spirit of Palestine. Prepare for a sensory journey that takes you through the most delicious traditional dishes and refreshing drinks, all prepared with love and passion. Please, come in, and let your senses celebrate every bite and every sip.

details about food in Resturent

Hummus: A creamy dip made from chickpeas, tahini (sesame paste), lemon juice, and garlic. It's a staple, perfect for dipping bread or vegetables.

Falafel: Deep-fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, often seasoned with herbs and spices. A popular and tasty street food.

Musakhan: chicken baked with caramelized onions, sumac (a tangy spice), and pine nuts, all served on a piece of flatbread called taboon. It's a flavorful and aromatic dish.

Maqluba: An "upside-down" rice dish with layers of meat (usually chicken or lamb) and fried vegetables like cauliflower, eggplant, and carrots. It's flipped onto a serving platter, making it a visually appealing and hearty meal.

Mansaf: A celebratory dish, particularly significant in Jordan but also found in Palestine. It features tender lamb cooked in a fermented dried yogurt sauce called jameed, served over rice or bulgur and topped with roasted nuts. It's often eaten communally.
